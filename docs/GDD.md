## Premise

Peaceful rural Japanese student roleplay for teens. Atmosphere of *Akebi's Sailor Uniform* -- quiet town, subtle customization, social rituals over grind.

## V0 Hypothesis

> Do players care about their student, their locker, their notes, and being seen by other players?

## V0 Loop (Target)

Morning arrival -> student name card -> locker -> customize locker -> send/receive note -> share snack -> school ends -> arcade (play/watch) -> locker/social follow-up.

Desks are classroom atmosphere props in V0, not assigned seats. Future backpack/keychain expression can use desk hooks later as its own slice.

## Two Social Centers

1. **Locker hallway** -- identity, notes, planning
2. **Arcade** -- active play, spectators, score-chasing

## Anti-Patterns

- Animal Crossing / Stardew clone
- Chaotic flex-avatar RP
- Paid random body/face rerolls
- Full school sim before social loop is fun

## Monetization Direction (Future)

Cosmetic/social purchases can exist later, but V0 should not add monetization UI or paid random identity.
